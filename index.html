Vehicle Maintenance Tips

  • 🔧 Change air & cabin filters regularly for clean air flow.
  • 🚘 Rotate tires every 6,000 miles.
  • 🔋 Test your battery every 6 months to avoid breakdowns.
  • 💨 Keep tires properly inflated for fuel savings.
  • 🧯 Carry a spare tire, jack, and an emergency kit at all times.
