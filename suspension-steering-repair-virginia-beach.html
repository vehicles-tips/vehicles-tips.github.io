Suspension & Steering FAQ

Do I need struts or just sway links?

Sway links cause sharp clunks over small bumps; worn struts cause bounce, nose-dive, and tire cupping. We test each component so you only replace whatâ€™s needed.

My wheel hums with speedâ€”bearing?

Likely. A hub bearing often grows louder when you load that side in a turn. We confirm with play checks and road test, then quote a hub replacement if needed.

Will new parts fix my uneven tire wear?

Replacing worn parts stops the cause; a post-repair alignment and correct tire pressure finish the job to protect new tires.
