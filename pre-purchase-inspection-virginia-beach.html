Buy Used with Confidence — We Check What Matters

Don’t gamble on a used car. Our mobile pre-purchase inspection gives you a clear picture of the vehicle’s true condition before you buy. We evaluate safety items, wear parts, fault codes, leaks, and road-test symptoms—then provide easy-to-understand notes so you can negotiate or walk away.

What’s Included (60–90 minutes typical)

Mechanical & Safety

  • Brakes: rotor thickness/spec, pad/shoe life, fluid condition
  • Suspension & steering: ball joints, tie rods, bushings, shocks/struts, play/noise checks
  • Leaks & underbody: engine, trans, coolant, PS, oil pan, lines & hoses, rust/corrosion
  • Tires: tread depth/wear pattern, inflation/TPMS, spare & tools
  • Alignment symptoms: pull, off-center wheel, abnormal tire wear, vibration

Electrical & Diagnostics

  • OBD-II scan: stored & pending codes with notes
  • Electronics: windows/locks, lights, wipers/washer, horn, dash warnings
  • Charging/starting: battery/alternator basics
  • HVAC: A/C cooling performance & heater output (visual/functional checks)
  • Fluids: levels & condition (engine oil, coolant, brake, PS/ATF when applicable)

⚠️ This is not a Virginia State Inspection; no stickers are issued. It’s a private, mobile evaluation to help you make a smart purchase decision.

FAQ

Do you test-drive?

Yes, when conditions and insurance allow. If a drive isn’t possible, we note why and focus on static checks and OBD-II data.

Can you scan ABS/Airbag modules?

We can scan basic codes and many ABS/SRS systems. Deep manufacturer-specific diagnostics may require a dealer-level tool; if so, we’ll advise.

Do you inspect EVs or hybrids?

We perform general inspections (tires, brakes, suspension, body, interior, chassis) and basic code scans. High-voltage battery health checks may require a brand specialist.
