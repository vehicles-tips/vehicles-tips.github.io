Common Symptoms

  • CEL on, flashing CEL (misfire)
  • Rough idle, stall, lack of power
  • Poor fuel economy, failed emissions
  • Hard starts, long crank, no start

  • Live-data evaluation (fuel trims, O2/AFR, MAF/MAP)
  • Misfire isolation; vacuum/smoke testing (when applicable)

Diagnostics FAQ

Will you clear my codes?

We can clear codes after repairs or to verify a fix. Clearing codes without fixing the cause is not recommended and may hide critical issues.

Can you diagnose intermittent problems?

Yes. We use freeze-frame data, wiggle testing, and repeated drive cycles to reproduce faults. Some intermittent issues may require staged testing.
