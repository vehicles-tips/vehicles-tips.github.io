Check Engine Light & Drivability

Common Symptoms

  • CEL on, flashing CEL (misfire)
  • Rough idle, stall, lack of power
  • Poor fuel economy, failed emissions
  • Hard starts, long crank, no start

What We Do

  • OBD-II scan & freeze-frame review
  • Live-data evaluation (fuel trims, O2/AFR, MAF/MAP)
  • Misfire isolation; vacuum/smoke testing (when applicable)
  • Targeted part/system testing before replacement

Heads-up: Clearing codes without fixing the cause only hides the problem temporarily. We diagnose first so you don’t waste money on guesswork.

ABS/Traction & Airbag/SRS Diagnostics

ABS/Traction Issues

  • Warning lights for ABS/ESC/Traction
  • Wheel-speed sensor or hub/bearing tone ring faults
  • Steering-angle sensor calibration needs
  • Hydraulic modulator/valve body faults (confirmed by tests)

Airbag/SRS Issues

  • SRS light on; stored crash data
  • Seat occupancy/pretensioner circuit faults
  • Clock spring/ribbon cable failures
  • Connector/corrosion checks under seats & modules

For safety systems we do scan, test, repair/replace components where feasible on-site. Some SRS resets/programming may require a partner shop — we’ll advise.

Electrical, Battery, Starting & Charging

We Test

  • Battery health & voltage drop
  • Alternator output & ripple (diode check)
  • Starter draw & cable integrity
  • Parasitic key-off drain
  • Blown fuses, shorts to ground, corroded grounds

Typical Fixes

  • Battery or alternator replacement
  • Starter or relay replacement
  • Repair of damaged wiring/connectors
  • Isolating and correcting aftermarket draw (lights, radios, add-ons)

Tip: Early signs like dim lights, slow crank, or intermittent no-start often point to charging/starting issues. Don’t wait until you’re stranded.

Diagnostics FAQ

Will you clear my codes?

We can clear codes after repairs or to verify a fix. Clearing codes without fixing the cause is not recommended and may hide critical issues.

Can you diagnose intermittent problems?

Yes. We use freeze-frame data, wiggle testing, and repeated drive cycles to reproduce faults. Some intermittent issues may require staged testing.

Do you work with customer-supplied parts?

Yes—please verify part numbers. Supplier handles parts warranty; our labor warranty applies to our work.
