1. Safety & Customer Parts

We may refuse installation of any part that could compromise safety or reliability. Please do not stand over technicians while they work.

↑ Back to top

2. What to Do If a Problem Arises

Contact us immediately if an issue occurs after our work. A diagnostic fee applies if it’s a new or unrelated problem, or if recommendations were ignored. If another shop touches the vehicle first, warranty is void.

↑ Back to top

3. Third-Party Repairs

Taking your vehicle to another workshop for the same issue before contacting us voids your warranty.

↑ Back to top

4. Parts & Workmanship

  • Workmanship warranty: 3 months or 3,000 miles (whichever comes first).
  • No warranty on customer-supplied parts. If a part fails, new labor charges apply.
  • A/C repairs: workmanship warranty applies. A/C recharges: no warranty or refunds.
  • We only discuss matters with the person on the original invoice.
↑ Back to top

5. Diagnostics

Diagnostics are not guaranteed to solve every issue in one repair. Incorrect customer diagnostics are not our responsibility. Pre-existing problems are excluded.

↑ Back to top

6. Fragile Parts

We are not responsible for breakages of plastics, wires, spark plugs, rubber, or metal parts that are brittle or worn.

↑ Back to top

7. Payments & Refunds

  • Payment is due in full upon completion.
  • Refunds require proof from a licensed mechanic and a printed receipt.
  • Stopping a job does not cancel labor charges.
↑ Back to top

8. Conduct Policy

We have zero tolerance for yelling, cursing, ignoring advice, or threats. Such behavior may result in immediate termination of service without refunds.

↑ Back to top

9. In-Depth Diagnostics

Cost: $95, applied toward same-day repairs (valid for 30 days if repairable). Excludes A/C diagnostics, non-fixable vehicles, or pre-purchase inspections. Non-refundable.

↑ Back to top

10. Part Pickup & Waiting Fees

Technician part pickup: $10 (within 10 miles) or $30 (20+ miles). Delays over 30 minutes: $50 per half hour unless communicated.

↑ Back to top

11. Cancellations

Cancellations within 72 hours are non-refundable. Refunds (when allowed) are returned to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

↑ Back to top

12. Inclement Weather

During inclement weather, services are paused and resume on the next business day.
