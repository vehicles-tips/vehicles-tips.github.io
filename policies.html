1. Safety & Customer Parts

We may refuse installation of any part that could compromise safety or reliability. Please do not stand over technicians while they work.

2. What to Do If a Problem Arises

Contact us immediately if an issue occurs after our work. A diagnostic fee applies if it’s a new or unrelated problem, or if recommendations were ignored. If another shop touches the vehicle first, warranty is void.

↑ Back to top

3. Third-Party Repairs

Taking your vehicle to another workshop for the same issue before contacting us voids your warranty.

↑ Back to top

4. Parts & Workmanship

  • Workmanship warranty: 3 months or 3,000 miles (whichever comes first).
  • No warranty on customer-supplied parts. If a part fails, new labor charges apply.
  • A/C repairs: workmanship warranty applies. A/C recharges: no warranty or refunds.
  • We only discuss matters with the person on the original invoice.
