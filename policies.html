Warranty & Policies

Vehicle-tips.com Policies, Warranties, Requirements, & Disclaimers

Last updated: 2025-08-27

1. Safety & Customer-Supplied Parts

We may refuse installation of any part that could compromise safe, reliable operation. Please do not stand over or interfere with the technician’s workspace during service.

↑ Back to top

2. What to Do If a Problem Arises

Contact us immediately if an issue occurs after recent work so we can schedule diagnostics. A diagnostic fee applies if the problem is new/unrelated or if recommendations were ignored. Any self-repair or third-party repair before our inspection voids applicable warranties.

↑ Back to top

3. Third-Party Repairs

Do not take your vehicle to another shop for the same issue before contacting us. If another party works on the vehicle first, your warranty with us is void.

↑ Back to top

4. Parts & Workmanship

  • Workmanship warranty: 3 months or 3,000 miles (whichever comes first).
  • We do not supply or warranty parts. If a customer-supplied part fails, new labor charges apply.
  • A/C repairs: workmanship warranty applies. A/C recharges: no warranty or refunds.
  • Service matters are discussed only with the person on the original invoice.
↑ Back to top

5. Diagnostics

Vehicles are complex; a single diagnosis may not resolve every issue. Full cost is owed even if an initial diagnostic does not fix all concerns. We are not responsible for pre-existing issues or incorrect customer self-diagnostics.

↑ Back to top

6. Fragile Components

We are not responsible for breakage of plastics, wires, spark plugs, metal, or rubber parts that are brittle, worn, or previously damaged by heat, fluids, or normal wear and tear.

↑ Back to top

7. Payments & Refunds

  • Payment in full is due upon completion. Stopping a job does not cancel labor charges.
  • Refunds require proof from a licensed mechanic and a verifiable printed receipt.
  • If service must pause due to parts/external issues, half the labor is due that day; the balance is due upon completion.
↑ Back to top

8. Conduct Policy

Zero tolerance for yelling, hostile behavior, profanity, or threats toward staff. Such behavior may result in immediate termination of service without refund and, if needed, involvement of authorities.

↑ Back to top

9. In-Depth Diagnostics

Cost: $95, applied toward same-day diagnostic repairs. Credit valid for 30 days if the vehicle is repairable. Excludes A/C diagnostics, non-fixable vehicles, vehicles advised for sale/trade, and pre-purchase inspections. Non-refundable. Additional diagnostics may be discounted.

↑ Back to top

10. Customer Part Pickup & Waiting Fees

Technician pickup fee: $10 (within 10 miles) or $30 (20+ miles). Customer delays beyond 30 minutes: $50 per additional 30 minutes unless communicated.

↑ Back to top

11. Cancellations & Refunds

Reschedule or cancel at least 72 hours before the appointment. Cancellations within 72 hours are non-refundable (including diagnostics, maintenance, inspections, A/C recharges, and virtual sessions). Refunds (when applicable) return to the original payment method within 5–7 business days, excluding weekends and holidays.

↑ Back to top

12. Inclement Weather

During inclement weather, operations pause and resume on the next business day.
